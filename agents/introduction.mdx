AI Agents are intelligent assistants that can work across all your connected applications, execute workflows, and handle complex business processes automatically. Choose the agent that best fits your needs.

## How Agents Work

AI Agents combine the power of artificial intelligence with your existing tools and workflows:

1. **App Integration**: Work seamlessly across all your connected applications
2. **Workflow Execution**: Execute complex multi-step processes automatically
3. **Intelligent Decision Making**: Make smart decisions based on context and data
4. **Team Collaboration**: Share agents with colleagues for consistent processes

## Ready to Get Started?

Choose Universal Agent for immediate productivity across all your apps, or create Custom Agents for specialized business processes. Both options give you the power of AI working alongside your existing tools and workflows.
