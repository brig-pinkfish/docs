## Welcome!

Pinkfish provides two powerful solutions to automate and enhance your enterprise work:

**AI Agents (Beta)** - Create custom AI agents tailored to your specific enterprise needs. These intelligent agents can leverage workflows to perform complex tasks, analyze data, and automate business processes. *Currently in beta with limited availability.*

**Workflows** - Build sophisticated automation workflows that can be triggered by various events, process data, and integrate with your existing tools and systems.

AI Agents can seamlessly use workflows to extend their capabilities, giving you the flexibility to create comprehensive automation solutions that adapt to your business requirements.
