**SSO** lets your organization sign into Pinkfish using a SAML identity provider — Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, Ping, JumpCloud, etc.

Open **Settings → SSO** (admin only).

## What SSO gives you

- **Centralized access control.** Remove a user in your IdP, they lose Pinkfish access.
- **MFA inheritance.** Users authenticate against your IdP's MFA policy.
- **Automatic provisioning.** New users land in Pinkfish with the right role without a manual invite.
- **Audit trail** through your IdP.

## What you'll need

- Admin access to your SAML IdP.
- Your Pinkfish organization domain (the email domain users sign in with).

## Setup at a glance

1. In **Settings → SSO**, copy Pinkfish's **Entity ID** and **ACS URL**.
2. Create a SAML application in your IdP with those values.
3. Paste your IdP's **Issuer URL**, **SSO URL**, and **X.509 certificate** back into Pinkfish.
4. Assign the app to the right group of users.
5. Test with a single user, then enforce SSO for the whole org.
