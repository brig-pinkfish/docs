The **Workflow Library** is the page you land on when you click **Workflows** in the top navigation. It's a flat list of every workflow in your organization, with filters, search, and the basic actions you need to get to work.

## What you can do from the Library

- **Search** by name or description — press `/` to focus the search bar.
- **Filter** by status (draft, released), tag, or owner.
- **Sort** by name, last updated, or last run.
- **Open** a workflow by clicking its row — this takes you into [Agent Mode](/workflows/overview) to edit it.
- **Run** a workflow directly from the row's action menu.
- **Duplicate** a workflow into a new draft. See [Duplication](/workflows/duplication).
- **Delete** one or several workflows at once.

## Creating a new workflow

Click **New workflow** in the top right. You'll be dropped into Agent Mode with a blank canvas. See [Agent Mode: Overview](/workflows/overview) for what to do next.
