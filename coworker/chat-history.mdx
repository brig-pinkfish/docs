Every Coworker chat is saved automatically. You can get back to an old conversation from:

- **The conversation sidebar** on the left side of Coworker, grouped by **Today / Yesterday / This week / Older**.
- **The `/coworker/history` page** — full-screen history browser with search and filters.

## What you can do

- **Search** across every past conversation by keyword.
- **Filter** by agent (@-mentioned in the chat).
- **Reopen** a chat and keep going where you left off.
- **Share** a chat with a teammate (see [Shared Chats](/coworker/shared-chats)).
