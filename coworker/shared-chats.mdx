You can share any Coworker conversation with a teammate in your organization. They'll see the full history, including any tool calls and attachments, and can continue the chat from where you left off.

## Sharing a chat

1. Open the chat you want to share from the conversation sidebar.
2. Click the **Share** icon in the chat header.
3. Pick one or more teammates.

The recipient sees a **Shared** badge on the chat in their sidebar.

## What gets shared

- The full message history.
- Any files attached to the conversation.
- Agent @-mentions and tool calls.

## What does NOT get shared

- **OAuth credentials.** If the chat uses an agent that calls Gmail, Salesforce, etc., the recipient must connect their own accounts before running those tools.
- **Private resources.** Datastores, filestores, and knowledge bases stay governed by their own [sharing](/organization/sharing) rules.
