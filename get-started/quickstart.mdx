The fastest way to see what Pinkfish does:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Go to [app.pinkfish.ai](https://app.pinkfish.ai) and sign in. If your company uses SSO, use the SSO button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Say hi in Coworker">
    Coworker is your chat with Pinkfish. Ask it to do something — e.g. "Summarize last week's Slack messages from #product." See [Coworker Overview](/coworker/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build a workflow">
    Click **Workflows → New workflow**. Describe what you want in plain English; Agent Mode drafts the nodes and wires them up. See [Workflows Overview](/workflows/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build an agent">
    Click **Agents → Create new agent**. Give it instructions and pick tools. Release it to show up in Coworker. See [Agents Overview](/agents/introduction).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your apps">
    Click **Tools → Connections** and authorize the integrations your workflows need (Gmail, Salesforce, Slack, whatever). See [Tools Overview](/tools/overview).
  </Step>
</Steps>
